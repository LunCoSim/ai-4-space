## Key Differences from EVM
- **Address Format**: Base58 string (e.g., `7xKX...`).
- **Speed**: Near-instant confirmation.
- **Fees**: Almost zero (~$0.0002).
- **Native Token**: Fees are always paid in **SOL**.
